About Us

We are Hopkins Sport Taekwondo Club (HST), a sports club team at Johns Hopkins University that practices and competes in the World Taekwondo (WT, formerly WTF) style of taekwondo. Our team is led by a WT-certified master from our parent school of the U.S. Taekwondo Academy (USTA), Master JY Kim. Our members range from beginners to black belts and test frequently to move up in belt rank. We offer free practices on campus and opportunities to compete regionally and nationally. As a member of our team, you can experience sparring and forms in both practice and competition. We are excited for you to join us and look forward to our time together!
